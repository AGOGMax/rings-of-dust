RACHEL takes the podium, the room quieting instantly. She scans the crowd, her gaze sharp and penetrating.
RACHEL Thank you. As many of you know, I’ve been reporting on the energy crisis and the efforts to break Alpha Corp’s stranglehold on Darkx. But what you don’t know is that I am more than just a reporter.
She pauses, letting the weight of her words sink in.
RACHEL I am Rachel, Grand Admiral of the Collective.
The room gasps in unison. COLE, SAM, RICARDO, and Bitboy exchange stunned looks.
COLE (whispering) Grand Admiral…
SAM (smiling) I had a feeling she was more than she seemed.
RACHEL (continuing) For years, I have worked undercover to gather information and support for our cause. Today, we stand victorious, not just because of one person, but because of the collective efforts of many. We have freed Darkx from the grasp of Alpha Corp, and now, we can ensure that no one will ever control our destiny again.
The crowd erupts in applause once more, louder and more fervent than before. RACHEL steps down from the podium and makes her way to COLE and the crew.
RACHEL (smiling warmly) Cole, Sam, Ricardo, Bitboy, you all played a crucial role in this victory. Thank you.
COLE (humbled) We couldn’t have done it without you, Grand Admiral.
RACHEL (laughing softly) Please, call me Rachel. We’re all part of the same fight.
SAM (stepping forward) Rachel, what’s our next move?
RACHEL (resolute) Now, we ensure that Darkx is distributed fairly and continue to dismantle any remnants of Alpha Corp’s influence. And we prepare for any pushback they might have.
GRACIE (via intercom) I will continue to support the Collective and monitor any threats. Together, we will ensure a brighter future.
RICARDO (grinning) Looks like we’re just getting started.
Bitboy (chitters in agreement, his blades retracting and extending excitedly)
RACHEL (placing a hand on COLE’s shoulder) You’ve all been through so much. Take a moment to rest, but stay ready. The fight for freedom and fairness is ongoing, but with the Collective united, we will prevail.
The crew nods, their spirits lifted by Rachel’s words. As they stand together in the Grand Hall, the sense of hope and determination is palpable. The journey has been long and hard, but with their newfound unity and the support of GRACIE, they are ready to face whatever challenges lie ahead.
