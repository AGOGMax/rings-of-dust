AntiGravity
 brings cryptocurrency, digital art, and DeFi tokenomics together into a robust ecosystem. The Antigravity NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) and Dark Matter Token ($DARK) ecosystem are rooted in the compelling narrative from our novel “Rings of Dust.”


MARKET ANALYSIS
The market for blockchain-based art and DeFi is rapidly expanding, driven by a growing demand for ecosystems that seamlessly merge innovative financial mechanisms with enhanced user experiences. This sector uniquely combines the creative potential of blockchain art, such as NFTs, with the innovations within DeFi to provide robust tokenomics and improved user interactions.


ECONOMIC INCENTIVES
Antigravity NFT holders enjoy various benefits, including exclusive giveaways, rewards, and programmed scarcity. Additionally, their rank within The Collective grants them varying degrees of influence, enhancing their experience and engagement within the ecosystem.

LIMITED MINTING WINDOWS
The 
AntiGravity
 project unfolds across 33 distinct minting phases. The inaugural minting phase will be 30 days, allowing newcomers to familiarize themselves with the AG minting process. Subsequent phases, from the second through the thirty-third, are streamlined to 15 days each. Spanning approximately 9.5 years, this timeline aims to elevate the value of the entire 
AntiGravity
 Universe progressively.


THE “EVIL ADDRESS”
The "Evil Address" mirrors the malevolent corporation in "Rings of Dust," embodying a relentless force determined to accumulate $DARK tokens and mint NFTs aggressively. This adversary heightens scarcity and introduces a unique challenge exclusive to our platform.


THE RAPTURE
After all 33 minting phases (approx. 9.5 years) are complete, any liquid $DARK tokens not being utilized in the ecosystem (i.e., minting an NFT, liquidity, and more) will be removed from supply and destroyed forever, lowering the total circulating supply. This one-time event will also remove “dust” (micro-amounts), and supply from dead (inactive) wallets.  


THE COLLECTIVE
The Collective’s innovative point system rewards community activities, enhancing user engagement and loyalty. The ongoing distribution of rewards and the structured ranking system ensure that community members are continually motivated to participate and contribute to the ecosystem's growth.
