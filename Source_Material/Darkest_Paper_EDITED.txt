What is $DARK?
$DARK is a PRC-20 token with a total supply of 1M tokens, no inflation, and programmed scarcity. It is also the only token used to mint Fuel Cell NFTs.

Components of 
AntiGravity

The three components of 
AntiGravity
 are:


Its story and art, inspired by "Rings of Dust"


The Dark Matter ($DARK) Token 


The Fuel Cell NFTs

How do Fuel Cells work?
Fuel Cell NFTs are minted at a 1:1 ratio with $$DARK tokens, and they have a supply cap of 1 million units. Users have 33 chances to mint the Fuel Cell NFTs and a 15-day window to mint each series. Fuel Cell NFTs also have 3 features: 


Artwork from "Rings of Dust"


Yield from the Treasury disbursed every 105 days


Lottery giveaways that take place in the 90 days following the minting phase

How is yield generated in 
AntiGravity
?
Yield is generated from the minting of Fuel Cells. 77% of the $$DARK tokens that are used in minting fund the Treasury. The Treasury pays yield every 105 days into the Fuel Cell NFTs. The yield can only be collected by breaking open (or “burning” in crypto terms) the Fuel Cell NFT. Once the Fuel Cell NFT is broken open (unwrapped) it no longer receives yield from the Treasury. Fuel Cell NFTs that are left unbroken continue to receive yield from the Treasury every 105 days.If you are familiar with the WAATSA NFTs from the 
Icosa
 project, this is a very similar concept. 

How does the Jackpot feature work?
20% of the $$DARK tokens used in minting go to funding the Jackpot address. When users mint a Fuel Cell NFT, they're automatically entered into 3 Lotteries drawings that take place during the following 90 days.
How does the Evil Address operate?The Evil Address has an unfair advantage in the 
AntiGravity
 game. Not only does it start with the majority of the supply (65,000 $DARK), but it also mints the majority of the Fuel Cell NFTs. This gives the Evil Address a higher chance of winning the lotteries and accruing the majority of the Treasury’s yield.

How does the Evil Address benefit $DARK and Fuel Cell holders?
The competitive nature of the Evil Address challenges other players in the game by removing the supply of $DARK from the market. This may increase demand for the tokens.

How are supply crisis events created?
Supply crises may arise from any of the following events:


The Evil Address hoarding tokens and NFTs 


Minting of the Fuel Cell NFTs 


Limited minting windows and limited minting periods


Players’ FOMO of getting into the next lotteries


A special, one-time event called "The Rapture" where all unutilized $$DARK tokens will be destroyed and removed from circulating supply following the final (33rd) minting phase
