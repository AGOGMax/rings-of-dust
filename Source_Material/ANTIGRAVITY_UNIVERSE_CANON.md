## The story properties

### 1. **The First Spark** *(prequel, complete novella)*
- **Format**: 37-minute animated narrated novella on YouTube
- **State**: **Complete and published.** ([0uT6ez_BX9M](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0uT6ez_BX9M))
- **Type**: Cosmic-horror prequel. First contact with DarkX in an abandoned Iceland mine, 50+ years before main book.
- **Cast**: Dr. Leora Henning, Anton Weiss, Arya Calder. The Sponsors / Directorate Oversight Division.
- **Function in canon**: Establishes that DarkX is sentient, was first encountered on Earth, caused a sealed-mine catastrophe, and migrated to / persists in Saturn's B-ring. *"Stone retains memory. Do not return."*
- **Tone**: Dark, restrained, cosmic horror. Leora's lab is the tonal benchmark.
- **Transcript**: `The_First_Spark_transcript.txt`

## Major worldbuilding canon (locked-in across the universe)

These are now constraints — change them at your peril:

- **DarkX is sentient.** Has been since first contact in Iceland. It studies humans through proximity. It manifests in stone, in dreams, in nanopowder.
- **DarkX is currently found only in Saturn's B-ring**, after Earth-based work was officially terminated post-Iceland.
- **The Directorate Oversight Division** is the proto-Federation/Alpha Corp. Black-coat secret-government project funders. They've been around 50+ years.
- **Gravity Sickness / Mitochondrial Degeneration** affects half the population. Cause is connected to gravitational shifts. (V2 prologue lore — keep it.)
- **The Federation** = government surface. **Alpha Corp** = corporate hand. **The Collective** = decentralized resistance.
- **The Collective sigil** is a water droplet inside a circle. Motto: *"No raindrop thinks it's the flood."*
- **IRiS** is the AI-via-nanopowder-injection system. It evolves into **Gracie** through proximity to DarkX.
- **Bitboy** is the last surviving "EM Cyber" — an early Federation experiment. The IRiS nanopowder was reverse-engineered from him.
- **"Rings of Dust"** vs **"DarkX"** vs **"Dark Matter"** vs **"Darker Matter"**:
  - "DarkX" = volatile plasma form (raw, direct from Saturn)
  - "Dark Matter" = stabilized crystal form (15-day shelf life, can be handled)
  - "Darker Matter" = the Collective's parallel economy / largest in-world node (and the IRL game brand)
  - "Rings of Dust" = poetic name for the substance's home (Saturn's rings) and the title of the main book
  - "Dark X" with a space is how it's first named in First Spark; the spelling contracts to "DarkX" by main-book era.
