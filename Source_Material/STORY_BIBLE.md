## 1. THE WORLD & SETTING

**Year**: 2043

**The energy economy**: The world runs on fuel cells powered by Darker Matter crystals. Darker Matter (processed from raw DarkX plasma) is the dominant energy source globally. Supply is controlled, pricing is managed, and the shortage that has defined the post-2030 era is not an accident.

**The core secret**: The shortage is a choice. Alpha Corporation discovered that Darker Matter cells, when properly managed over time, produce exponentially more energy than they advertise. They classified the findings, destroyed viable product to suppress supply, and built a shadow government around the gap. The proof was built into the technology before the technology was handed over. The lock was made before the key. Everything else was patience.

**Institutions**:
- **Alpha Corporation**: The true governing power. Founded by Klaus Verguld in 1991. Marcus Verguld's current instrument. Runs money through the Federation. Federation budget is effectively Alpha Corp money. "The Federation is crawling with MY employees." (Marcus)
- **The Federation**: NASA equivalent — real government, real scientists, real Astros. Captured at top levels by Alpha Corp. Not uniformly corrupt (Vance Black was proof). Hierarchy extends to the Chancellor level (DeMint).
- **The Collective**: Distributed resistance network. Volunteers, scientists, former Federation personnel. Not an army. A living network — founded to build the world they want inside the walls of the world that exists.

## 3. THE SCIENCE — CANONICAL FUEL CELL MECHANICS

*This is the authoritative version. Any contradictions in other reference documents defer to this section.*

### What Darker Matter Is

- **DarkX** (raw form): plasma, obsidian-black, volatile, almost sentient. The writing rule: DarkX is never just a fuel source. Every scene involving it should carry the suggestion it is aware of what is being done to it — and that it is patient.
- **Processing**: DarkX is stabilized via salt compounds + liquid nitrogen + negative pressure → produces **Darker Matter crystals** (solid, stable, usable energy source)

### The Standard Fuel Cell Cycle (11 Days)

1. Darker Matter crystals sealed inside a fuel cell (nitrogen environment, negative pressure)
2. Crystals dissolve to vapor → vapor forms vortex → vortex charges the cell
3. Day 11: mist clears; new crystals form on the **exterior** of the cell base
4. Harvest those exterior crystals → transfer to a fresh cell → repeat

### The Sync Period (Days 0–11)

- Each newly assembled cell (assembled within 11 days of DarkX stabilization) undergoes a resonance phase
- Felt as a low-frequency shifting through anyone nearby — like a radio scanning through static, searching for a signal
- On Day 11 when sync completes: silence. Something has completed.
- Elsa Klinge's description: "The feeling of a new node reaching toward the network."

### Day 11 — The Jackpot (One-Time, One Cell Per Grid)

- In a 740-cell Grid, when sync completes, **ONE cell at random** receives a massive one-time exterior crystal deposit — orders of magnitude larger than a normal 11-day harvest
- Random. Does not repeat. Cannot be predicted.
- "You get one Jackpot per new Grid. You just don't know where it's going to land."
- Both Ray (in his empty Alpha lab) and Alpha Corp (years earlier in their own lab) independently said "Jackpot" when they first observed this phenomenon. Ray did not know Alpha had named it the same thing.

### Day 33 — The Lock

- A cell **locked** (sealed, inaccessible) past Day 33: internal crystal growth accelerates to **10× the rate of an unlocked cell**
- This is INTERIOR growth — the exterior casing looks unchanged
- Alpha Corp classified this finding. They never published it.

### Day 180 — The Threshold

- At Day 180: interior jumps from 10× to **100×** the rate of an unlocked cell
- **Every cell in the Grid** (all 740) begins producing exterior crystal deposits on an 11-day cycle, continuously, until unlocked
- This is the economic bomb: unlimited new Darker Matter from existing units

### Unlock

- Unlock a cell at any point: all enhanced properties cease within hours
- Interior returns to baseline. Exterior 11-day cycle stops.
- A cell is either locked and producing, or unlocked and finished. No partial state.
- Alpha harvested the 11-day exterior deposits and reset. They never locked long enough to find what was underneath.

### The Sacrifice Mechanic (Void-Walker Use)

- Darker Matter crystals and charged fuel cells can be deliberately pushed into DarkX (described as a void — a black hole that nothing comes back from)
- When sacrificed into an active DarkX field, the DarkX absorbs it
- Something comes back — not immediately, not proportionally, but eventually, in a form that defies ordinary accounting of matter and energy
- The colony's void: at the end of the east tunnel, a pressure membrane separating habitation from open space where DarkX fields exist
- **Void-Walker interpretation**: the returns are not material — they are ancestral (energy feeds ancestors who reincarnate into the bloodline)
- **Loyalist interpretation**: the returns ARE material (asteroid deflection via gravity differential — Elsa's math proves it)
- Both factions are observing the same phenomenon and explaining it differently
- Writing rule: both interpretations remain valid simultaneously. The text does not resolve this.

### 369 Test Runs

Ray ran 369 test runs at Station Libre. 369 identical results. Six operators. Triplicate verification. This is the proof Rachel Vayes reviews before the Grand Hall hearing.

## 7. THE VERGULD DYNASTY

### Family Tree & Founding Structure

The belief predates the Verguld name. It started generations before Wilhelm, with his grandmother. Two streams flow from her original teaching. Everything else is downstream.

**THE ORIGINAL SOURCE — Wilhelm's Grandmother**
She started the cult/religion generations before The First Spark. The belief: your acts and deeds bless your bloodline going forward; you can reincarnate into your own bloodline; the right rituals ensure future generations receive what you sow. Her rituals include a prophecy: a descendant of this bloodline will one day come to the place where the sacrifice is made. Everything begins here.

**ACCUMULATION STREAM (Verguld dynasty)**:
Wilhelm Verguld takes the belief and redirects it toward ownership and empire.
Wilhelm → Klaus (patient, generational control) → Marcus (theatrical accumulation).

**SACRIFICE STREAM (colony tradition)**:
Followers of the original grandmother's tradition carry the sacrifice path across generations.
Eventually: Elsa Klinge founds the asteroid colony.

**ELSA KLINGE — prophetess, not biological grandmother**
Elsa is NOT Jill's biological grandmother. She is a prophetess within the tradition that began with Wilhelm's grandmother.
She founded the colony on both the science (dark matter stabilization) and the belief (sacrifice, void, the ancestral return).
Her notebooks contain a prophecy: the offspring of the founding bloodline will come to the colony. The notebooks wait for that person.
Maiden name: Klinge. How she connects to the original tradition is a deliberate redaction — to be revealed later.
After Elsa's death: Tyler Durden builds his religion on top of her science and her prophecy.

**JILL — the prophesied descendant**
Jill is a descendant of Wilhelm's grandmother's founding bloodline — on a branch that never took the Verguld accumulation path and never found the colony until now.
She is NOT Elsa's biological granddaughter.
Petra says "You're not her granddaughter" — technically correct. Elsa's notebooks are not for a granddaughter. They are for the fulfillment of a prophecy.
Petra is right for the wrong reason. Jill IS the person the notebooks were waiting for.
Her family name, when traced, connects back to the founding bloodline. This is the maiden name reveal.
Elsa's prophecy was always about her. She came to the colony by accident. She was always going to come.

### The Verguld Belief (Canonical)

Wilhelm's grandmother taught him: *If you work hard enough, perform the right rituals, and make the right sacrifices, you will be blessed in your bloodline going forward. Your acts and deeds will ensure future generations reap what you sow. You can reincarnate into your own bloodline — what you do now comes back to you through your descendants.*

The grandmother performed a ritual. Wilhelm watched. He kept the belief but redirected it toward accumulation and ownership — "I will build an empire so my bloodline inherits power."

**The fork — same belief, opposite directions**:

- **Accumulation path (Verguld dynasty)**: Give nothing to the void. Take everything. Ensure the bloodline through material inheritance. Klaus expressed it as patient, generational control. Marcus expressed it as theatrical accumulation.
- **Sacrifice path (Elsa Klinge / Void-Walkers)**: Give back to the void. The void returns what matters. The bloodline is blessed through what you give, not what you keep.

*"The grandmother and the grandfather drank from the same well and carried what they found there in opposite hands."*
