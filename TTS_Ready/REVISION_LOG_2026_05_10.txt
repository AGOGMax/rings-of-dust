NEW CHAPTERS

  Ch10a_Max_At_Alpha
    Max working at Alpha's Dark Matter Stabilization lab after Polaris.
    Alpha can stabilize Dark X for 11-day windows only. Max takes 30g home
    (unauthorized). Overnight the apartment fuel cell array charges to levels
    it shouldn't. He works for 33 days, verifies crystal growth on exterior
    casing, charts the yield. Takes results to Alpha. Shows them. Room goes
    quiet. He's fired that afternoon. Walks home still carrying his desk box.
    Red and Squirrel knock.
    Slots after Ch10 / before Ch10b in reading order.

  Ch10b_Max_And_The_Flywheel (existing)
    Already in place — Max extraction from apartment.

  Ch10c_Max_In_Panama
    Max arrives at Tocumen International — disoriented, too hot, wearing a
    jacket in 90-degree morning. Gets on a pedicab (NOT a cab — Red's note
    said DON'T TAKE A CAB). Driver zips him through the city: lanes advisory,
    signals in negotiation, a bus that always blows its horn. Stops at a door
    painted green. The green of something that's been green long enough to
    mean it. He gives the driver all his money; driver gives some back. Max
    stands in front of the door and thinks about Alpha's face. Knocks.
    Slots after Ch10b in reading order.

  Ch21a_The_Zero_G_Flywheel
    Max in Panama (14 months in), has run the 180-day experiment 11 times.
    Realizes gravity is the missing variable — in zero-g, no preferred convection
    direction, the crystal growth should skip the plateau entirely. Brings the
    idea to Red. Red makes calls. Nine days later: the power spike. Three fuel
    cells active simultaneously on day 33 — ten-times charge rate — flags the
    city infrastructure grid. Alpha has people inside that department. Red gets
    word in 4 hours. Two hours moving equipment, two hours moving Max. "Two
    weeks. I moved the timeline up." Max packs his notebook. Drives through
    Panama City dark, past the heron, past the green door, out to the highway.
    Slots between Ch21 and Ch21b.

  Ch21b_Max_And_The_Station
    Max takes Collective transport to Station Libre. 2.5g burn — first time.
    11 people on the station. Whiskey Slap game in week three: spin the bottle,
    whoever it points to takes a shot and gets slapped. Slap helps the burn
    hit in low-g. On day 180 the 33-day plateau breaks upward — crystal mass
    on exterior of fuel cell jumps more than the previous 60 days combined.
    Max gets Dae-jung. "The shortage was a choice." Rachel arrives 4 months
    before the Grand Hall hearing, same transport route. He shows her the
    180-day curve across 7 test units. She says: "I know." He hadn't
    expected that.
    Slots after Ch21 / before Ch21c in reading order.

  Ch21c_Vance_At_The_Black_Site
    Vance uses Federation credentials to access the Henderson, Nevada facility
    — a strip mall facade above 7 underground levels. Federation built it for
    records; Alpha has quietly cleared it. Sublevel 5 clerk Chen: the fuel
    cell procurement file was cleared March 14th, full vault, authorized by
    "Federation Representative Rachel Vayes." Vance drives 30 min north on 215,
    pulls over. Thinks about what it means that DeMint didn't know. Calls
    Marcus. Goes to Alpha's 32nd floor. "The records were cleared." Marcus:
    "They were a liability." Eight years. "I know." That's all.
    Vance drives himself to a clinic. Sits in the parking lot 20 minutes.
    Then goes in. That part mattered.
    Slots before Ch20 in reading order (Ch20 references his death as past).
